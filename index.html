Gesture Control Drone

Aeromodelling Club · Computer Vision · Drone Control

Overview

Developed a quadcopter that can be controlled using hand gestures. A camera and OpenCV pipeline detect gestures and map them to flight commands such as take-off, hover, rotate, and directional motion.

Design & Hardware

  • Quadcopter frame with brushless motors and ESCs.
  • Flight controller (Betaflight/custom) for stabilization.
  • Laptop running real-time OpenCV gesture recognition.
  • Wireless communication link between ground station and drone.

Code & Control Logic

The system has two layers: a Python/OpenCV node that classifies gestures, and a firmware layer that converts high-level commands into stable motor outputs, respecting attitude and safety constraints.

Code: Gesture-Controlled Drone Repository

EMG Based Bionic Arm

Biomedical Robotics · EMG · Mechatronics

Overview

Ongoing development of a bionic arm driven by EMG signals. The arm interprets muscle activity from the forearm to control finger, wrist, and elbow motion for assistive or prosthetic use.

Design & Hardware

  • Multi-joint arm CAD modelled in Fusion 360 / SolidWorks.
  • Servo-based actuation for fingers and joints.
  • EMG sensors for muscle activation measurement.
  • Microcontroller for real-time filtering and control.

Signal & Control

EMG signals are filtered, rectified, and thresholded to detect intent, which maps to discrete gestures such as open hand, close hand, and wrist rotation. Future versions target proportional and adaptive control.
